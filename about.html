WE Are

Innovating with Nature for a Sustainable Future

At Bio10, we believe natural solutions are the key to a sustainable world. Our biotechnology harnesses nature’s power to create eco-friendly products that reduce waste and improve efficiency. Committed to innovation and environmental impact, we aim to build a better future across industries

Our Mission

At Bio10, our mission is to drive innovation through natural solutions that promote sustainability. We aim to reduce waste, enhance plant health, and create eco-friendly technologies that benefit both industries and the environment. Through continuous research and development, we strive to make a lasting, positive impact on our planet.

We Established in 2019

Bio10 was established in 2019 in Queensland, Australia. Our founders and technical team have a combined 30 years of expertise in biochemistry, food technology, biotechnology, nanotechnology and aquaculture. This multi-faceted background of skills and knowledge puts us in a unique position and promotes visionary solution solving.

Our Goal

Our goals are to advance sustainable biotechnology by developing natural, efficient solutions across diverse sectors, including waste management, plant health, and beyond. We aim to lead the way in reducing environmental impact, supporting industries in adopting eco-friendly practices, and fostering innovation that benefits both people and the planet, without being limited to specific applications.

Some Facts

Bio10 combines expertise in biotechnology, environmental science, and sustainable solutions. Driven by innovation, we create cutting-edge technologies that benefit industries and the environment, always pushing the boundaries of sustainable progress.
