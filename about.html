Our Mission

We are an agrobiotechnology company intent on changing the current standard practices and approaches within a wide variety of industries. Regardless of application, our aim is to ensure a sustainable outcome with a strong emphasis on closed-loop recycling.

We Established in 1984

Bio10 was established in 2019 in Queensland, Australia. Our founders and technical team have a combined 30 years of expertise in biochemistry, food technology, biotechnology, nanotechnology and aquaculture. This multi-faceted background of skills and knowledge puts us in a unique position and promotes visionary solution solving.

Our Goal

Presently, many countries around the globe have been working towards reducing their heavy dependency on synthetic chemical pesticides to ensure safer, more sustainable and effective natural-based solutions to the problem.

Bio10 aims to tackle these issues head-on and provide a range of effective and natural-based products, to slowly lower the amounts of synthetic chemical pesticide usage globally.
